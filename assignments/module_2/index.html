Genres

Comedy

Horror
Perhaps something just feels wrong or maybe everything was going well until a character suddenly saw or heard something. Either way, as shivers run down your spine, you know that it is time to run!
Tragedy
As you watch these characters' lose their footing in life and roll downhill, you wonder what they did to deserve this. Keep a box of tissues ready.
